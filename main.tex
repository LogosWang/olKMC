\section{Simulation Method}

In this study, we employ an off-lattice kinetic Monte Carlo (KMC) model to predict the diffusion and oxidation behavior of materials. The KMC model allows us to simulate the stochastic processes of atomic diffusion and chemical reactions at the atomic scale, providing insights into the mechanisms driving these phenomena. We consider the interactions between atoms and the effects of temperature and concentration gradients on diffusion rates. The model is calibrated using experimental data and validated against known diffusion coefficients and oxidation rates.
